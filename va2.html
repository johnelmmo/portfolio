JOHN LIMIAC
WORK ABOUT RESUME

Vancouver Aquarium

Improving the overall guest experience by providing spatial design solutions.

Role

Co-UX Researcher, Visual Designer

Summary

Conducted user research with Vancouver Aquarium's admissions staff to create design solutions that improve guests' overall experience during their visit at the site.

Skills

UX Research, Visual Design

Timeline

This research was conducted in a 3-month period during the Spring 2018 semester. During this time, we conducted on-site visits, staff interviews, and participatory workshops to gather data.
