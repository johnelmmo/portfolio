About Me

I am a goal-driven ux/ui designer focused on analyzing intricate interactions to provide meaningful and positive experiences for the users.

Previously, I worked with a cross-functional team at TTT Studios in creating a marketplace app from scratch, aimed to transform the online marketplace into a safer, bigger, and better place for its users. I have also worked with the Guest Services staff at the Vancouver Aquarium and conducted UX Research with 3 other students to improve the overall guest experience during their initial visit to the aquarium.

I bring passion, new ideas, and energy to the table. I strive to continue learning everyday — in my own free time reading design blogs and articles or at the workplace learning from my team.

Whenever I’m not in front of my laptop, I’m either in my hiking shoes somewhere in the mountains or behind my camera capturing stories.
